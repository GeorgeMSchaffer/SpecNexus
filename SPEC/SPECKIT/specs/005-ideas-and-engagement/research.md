# Research: Ideas and Engagement

## Key Decisions
- ideas require title and description
- completed ideas remain editable and collaborative
- tags are normalized, org-scoped, and merged on concurrent duplicates
- mentions resolve by email in ideas and comments
- only the user who cast an upvote can remove it

## Risk Areas
- deterministic tag normalization and merge behavior
- same-organization mention resolution across ideas and comments
- preserving role rules for Read Only and board-configured User status changes
